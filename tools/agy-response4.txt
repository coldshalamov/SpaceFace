### Proposed Bacigalupi Element: Catalytic Silt ("Silt")
Instead of oxygen or water, the system is organized around **Catalytic Silt**—a heavy rare-earth silicate slurry that coats the interior channels of recycler grids. 
* **The Decay Loop:** Silt degrades over time as it is poisoned by trace moisture, industrial fumes, and organic lung waste. As Silt goes inert, turning into pale **"Chalk"**, stations slowly succumb to the **"Slow Gray"**—a chronic, non-lethal carbon dioxide poisoning that induces metabolic lethargy and brain fog.
* **The Heist Link:** This ties directly to the Ritchie-style reveal in [NPC-ECOLOGY.md](file:///C:/Users/93rob/Documents/GitHub/SpaceFace/docs/worldbuilding/story/NPC-ECOLOGY.md). The 12.4 tons of "titanium alloy" the player hauled in B0 was the Pit's primary recycler catalyst grid pre-loaded with high-grade Silt. By stealing it, they triggered the Pit's metabolic decay, causing the productivity drops that Director Vale used to justify cutting the Pit's maintenance budget.
